About Sam




Sam Sulek (born 7 February 2002; age 21) is a US Tiktok star and fitness influencer. He became a social media sensation after uploading viral fitness content. To date, he has accumulated over half a million followers on the short video-sharing app. He is currently 5 foot 11 at 230 pounds as he is trying to lose weight by cutting. Apart from Tiktok, he actively shares content on YouTube and Instagram as well.
